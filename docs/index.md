## How to Delete All User Data

All data in TinyTimeTracker is stored **locally on your device** and not on any external servers.
To delete a specific tracker account:

1.  Select the time tracking account you wish to delete.
2.  Click on the context menu (usually three dots) in the top right corner.
3.  Choose the "**Delete Account**" option.
4.  Confirm your decision in the dialog box that appears.

To delete all app data, you can simply uninstall the TinyTimeTracker app from your device.

## Translation Guidelines

I appreciate contributions to translate TinyTimeTracker!

To contribute a translation:

1.  Download the `strings.xml` file from our GitHub repository: [https://github.com/firebirdberlin/tinytimetracker/blob/master/android/res/values/strings.xml](https://github.com/firebirdberlin/tinytimetracker/blob/master/android/res/values/strings.xml).
2.  Replace the English terms with your translations, ensuring the XML tag structure remains intact.
3.  Send the translated `strings.xml` file to [stefan.fruhner+tinytimetracker@googlemail.com](mailto:stefan.fruhner+tinytimetracker@googlemail.com).
